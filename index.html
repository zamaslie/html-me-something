Livin' La Vida Leo 🐶

My name is Leo and I'm a 6 month old Lab Mix from South Florida. My mom and dad rescued me when I was six weeks old and I've been Livin La Vida Leo ever since!

My First Day Home!

I'll remember it for the rest of my life! I was sitting inside of my kennel at the Broward County Humane Society when a volunteer came and picked me up and brought me to a room. There was a couple inside who started making weird noises the second they saw me. I guess they were excited to see me, because I went home with them! And boy was I lucky! My new mom and dad were so nice and they had so many toys and delicious treats for me the minute I got home.

My First Walk!

My first walk around my new neighborhood was pretty exciting. I'm a pretty big deal around that area because I'm so cute, so you can imagine how many heads I turned this day.

Meeting My Grandparents!

I have two sets of grandparents. I haven't met my dad's parents yet but I've met my mom's parents and I love them soooo much. My grandpa said he didn't want any dogs in his house but when he saw me he changed his mind right away.

My First Visit to a Restaurant!

My parents love to go out to eat and they always try to pick places to take me with them! This is from my first visit to a restaurant called Colada Cuban Cafe in Fort Lauderdale. The staff was very nice and they gave me some treats and water! Yum!

Most pups are scared of the vet, but not me! Everytime I go they give me treats and everybody pets me. There's nothing I love more than treats and pets. My parents were really excited this day because apparently I gained weight. I'm officially 16.3 pounds!!
